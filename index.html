Benefits of Sports

If your child has the potential to utilize physical energy towards Sports, let him cherish that phase, let him evolve as an athlete, let him live his life with utmost love. Even if your child will not be able to leave mark in Sports as a career, don’t let it be rooted out of his/her life. It provides give long term benefits like:
1 - Overall development - Physical, mental and spiritual.
2 - Mind functioning also gets improved due to better blood circulation.
3 - Sports quota will definitely help you get Government job, not only Railways, but many other SSC jobs that consider Sports Quota.
4 - Sports also give scope to become great umpire, referee, commentator etc., in case your luck will not work as a player.
